What I Treat

Expert Care for Every Recovery

From acute sports injuries to long-term occupational rehabilitation, every treatment plan is tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Sports Injuries

Assessment, treatment and rehabilitation of sports-related injuries. Get back to peak performance safely and efficiently.

🦴

Back Pain

Targeted therapy for acute and chronic back pain. Evidence-based techniques to reduce pain and prevent recurrence.

Arthritis Support Group

About

Physiotherapy Done Right
